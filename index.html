HOW A COUNTRY'S LIVING STANDARD IMPACTS ITS DEVELOPMENT FACTORS

The living standard in a country refers to the overall quality
of life enjoyed by its citizens. This concept is influenced by a
wide range of factors, including economic, social, health
and environmental conditions. It is believed that there is a
strong and reciprocal relationship between living standards and
the development of a nation.

Cost of Living Around the World

The living standard has a significant impact on the cost of living for citizens of a country.
Higher living standard indicates a higher cost of living in the country.
Hence, most developed countries has higher cost of living compared to developing countries.

Income Comparison between Countries from Different Status

Living standard of a country also relates closely with their annual income in average.
It is believed that developed countries that has higher annual income in average
has a better living standard compared to developing countries that has
lower annual income in average.

Exploring Physical Health of Citizens Around the World

The living standard of a population is significantly impact by the healthcare system
of a country. It is believed that countries with a better living standard often results
in a better healthcare system that contribute to a healthier
population and sustainable healthcare environment.

Exploring Mental Health of Citizens between Countries from Different Status

However, it is also believed that countries with higher living standard often leads to
a stress and burdening environment that might cause serious mental health problem
in individuals. Hence, it is crucial to take immediate actions against improving
the mental health of countries if necessary.
